Home | Merits

There are many benefits of associating with the international Board of environmental Health and Safety Inc. USA.The main merits of the IBOEHS program include the following.

  1. IBOEHS safety programs are OSHA standard-based programs 
  2. IBOEHS programs are more industry-specific 
  3. IBOEHS programs are more applied in nature 
  4. IBOEHS programs are comparatively significantly less expensive  
  5. The MCQ types of examination test individual's accurate knowledge on the subject 
  6. IBOEHS tests learner's subject knowledge than English language ability 
  7. The registered status renewal system will ensure an individual's continuous learning process.
  8. All learners receive a quarterly IBOEHS newsletter free of cost.
  9. All IBOEHS certificate holders receive 10% off on the course fee for their following IBOEHS qualification.
  10. The Center accreditation fee is relatively low.
  11. IBOEHS supplies all course materials free of cost. So the centers need not invest time and expertise in developing course materials. 
  12. IBOEHS follows simple procedures in terms of accreditation and center management.
  13. Can book examinations with IBOEHS at short notice.
  14. Centre accreditation renewal fee, when compared to other awarding bodies, is significantly low. 
  15. IBOEHS is one of the rarest awarding bodies Offering Registered status to its learners.
