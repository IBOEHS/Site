  • Oil & Gas Upstream Safe Worker
  • Registered Environmental Officer®

The certificate in Oil and gas upstream safe worker program is designed to provide required skill to the people having responsibility for safety of people at ON shore & OFF shore oil and gas facilities. The program will be an ideal choice for people working in oil rigs, platforms, vessels, refineries and processing units including oil and gas construction and shut down projects.

Highlights of the course

  • Oil & Gas Upstream Operations
  • How Oil Drilling Works
  • Types of Drilling
  • Drilling Rig Types          
  • Rig Personnel
  • Explore the Potential Hazards – Oil & Gas Well Drilling
  • Hydrogen Sulfide
  • Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA)
  • Liquefied Natural Gas
  • Offshore Rig Safety

Target Group

People working in oil rigs, platforms, vessels, refineries, and processing units, including oil and gas construction and shut down projects. Or those who are planning to work in any of the above industries

Eligibility : No any prerequisite. However good written and  oral command in English Language will be an added advantage

Duration : 04 days including Examination

Examination : The examination consists fifty multiple-choice

Pass percentage : 80% 

What will students receive on successful completion? 

IBOEHS accredited certificate in oil and gas upstream safe worker Certificate

The Registered Environmental Officer provides recommendations on environmental protection which includes prevention of pollution to air, Water, and Soil from all possible man-made contamination. An REO will be capable of advising the organization in terms of Collection, Storage, Transportation and Disposal of hazardous and non-hazardous waste materials and protection of the environment through sustainable development.

Highlights of the course

  • Human Anatomy and Physiology
  • Earth Science
  • Recognition of Environmental Hazards
  • Evaluation of Environmental Hazards
  • Environmental Control Measures
  • Respiratory Protection
  • Confined Spaces
  • Ergonomics
  • Hearing Conservation
  • Toxic Awareness
  • Handling Hazardous Materials and Waste

Target Group

Safety Managers, Safety Officers, Environmental Monitors, Environmental officers, HAZMAT Handlers, Material coordinator & People looking for Environmental related jobs  

Eligibility: Graduates/Safety officer with Responsibility for Environmental Management

Duration: 08 days including Examination

Examination : The examination consists of 120 multiple-choice questions – online   examination 

Pass percentage : 80%

What will students receive on successful completion? 

IBOEHS accredited REO Certificate & REO wallet card Successful candidate will be    entitled to use the REO initial behind their name. Validity of Wallet card is 2 yrs. Every 2 years  wallet card need to be renewed
