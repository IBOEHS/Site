The Occupational Healthcare Nurse program is designed for people with nursing background and desirous to become industrial nurse. This is an ideal program for people desirous to work in the field of nursing and occupational safety together.

Highlights of the course

  • The Occupational Health Nurse
  • Industrial Health
  • Health Hazards
  • Chemical Hazards
  • Physical Health Hazards
  • Biological Health Hazards
  • Food Safety
  • Personal Protective Equipment
  • Confined Space s
  • Control of Hazardous Energy
  • Fire Prevention and Protection
  • Life Safety
  • Hazardous Materials and Waste
  • Ergonomics
  • Sanitation
  • Staying Fit to Work
  • Substance Abuse Awareness

Target Group

General nurses, Ex defense Medical Assistants. Health workers 

Eligibility: Nursing background- Diploma in nursing or above

Duration: 04 days including Examination

Examination: The examination consists of  eighty seven  multiple-choice questions – online   examination

Pass percentage : 80%

What will students receive on successful completion? 

IBOEHS accredited OHN Certificate.

The Infection Control Management program is designed to give required knowledge against biological and other infections at work place to the people responsible for self and others protection against possible agents at work place. The program mainly concentrates on identification and mitigation of infection causing agents at workplace. 

Highlights of the course

  • Need for Health care Infections Control
  • Populations at Risk
  • Infections Associated with Invasive Procedure
  • Common Infections
  • Routes of Transmission
  • Elements of an Infection Control Program
  • Infection Control Techniques
  • Collection of Sputum Specimen
  • Employee Exposure Assessment Form
  • Sterilization and Disinfection of Equipment
  • Storage and Handling of Equipment
  • Environmental Management
  • Waste Treatment
  • Disposal of Medical Waste

Target Group

General nurses, Safety professionals, Waste handlers, Environmental officers 

Eligibility : Graduates/RSO/RSM/RSP/REO

Duration : 04 days including Examination

Examination : The examination consists of  twenty  multiple-choice questions – online   examination

Pass percentage : 80%

What will students receive on successful completion? 

IBOEHS accredited Infection Control Management Certificate

The Food Safety Management program is designed to provide required skill to the people having responsibility to advice people on dietary requirements and preservation of food and associated items in a hospital environment. The program also highlights personal hygiene practices in order to prevent the spread of infection and cross contamination. 

Highlights of the course

  • Impact of Foodborne Illnesses
  • Hospital Food Safety Manager Responsibilities
  • Eleven International Food Safety Icons
  • Foodborne Illness
  • How the threat to health starts                                                
  • Biological hazards                                                         
  • Physical hazards                                                            
  • Chemical hazards                                                          
  • Preventing Foodborne Illnesses


Target Group

Safety professionals, Food handlers, Cold storage staff, Camp bosses, anybody handling food and associated substances 

Eligibility: No any prerequisite. However good written and oral command in English Language will be an added advantage 

Duration: 05 days including Examination

Examination : The examination consists of  Twenty multiple-choice questions – online   examination

Pass percentage : 80%

What will students receive on successful completion? 

IBOEHS accredited FSM Certificate.

The Registered Healthcare Safety Officer® (RHSO) is knowledgeable of the fundamentals of occupational Health and Safety in the healthcare environment including hospitals, old age homes, jeweneval homes, jails. And destitute homes. The RHSO is generally responsible for advising the management in terms of care and protection of inmates from all possible hazards and risks pertaining to above industry. 

Highlights of the course

  • Safety Terminology
  •  Management’s Role in Safety
  •  Safety Committee
  •  Communicating Safety Effectively
  •  Safety Training
  •  Hazard Recognition and Control Measures
  •  Specific Hazards and Risk Associated with Hospitals
  •  Specific Hazards and Risk Associated with Old Age Homes
  •  Specific Hazards and Risk Associated with Jeweneval Homes 
  •  Specific Hazards and Risks Associated with Destitute Homes. 
  •  Hazardous Materials Management
  •  Ergonomics 
  •  Violence Management
  •  Infection Control
  •  Palliative Care

Target Group

Safety professionals, Nurses, Rig Medics , and Hospital administrators 

Eligibility : Graduate in Zoology /Nursing qualified/Para medical qualified/Safety Officer

Duration: 06 days including Examination for virtual classroom. E learning mode of study has 06 month time to complete the course

Examination : The examination consists of multiple-choice questions – online   examination

Pass percentage : 80%

What will students receive on successful completion? 

IBOEHS accredited RHSO Certificate & RHSO wallet card.

Muscular-skeletal disorders (MSD) are an increasing problem for all industries. With an understanding of the principles of ergonomics many of the health hazards that lead to a MSD can be identified and corrective action taken before they become a problem.

Highlights of the course

  •  
  • Defining Ergonomics
  • Ergonomic Risk Factors
  • Ergonomic Disorders        
  • Ergonomic Disorder Assessment           
  • Ergonomic Injury Prevention        
  • Ergonomic Program Development
  • Posture
  • Non-Powered Tools
  • Manual Lifting
  • Vibration
  • Oil & Gas Industry
  • Office Ergonomics

Target Group

Safety professionals, nurses, Rig medics

Eligibility : Graduate in Zoology /Nursing qualified/Para medical qualified/Safety Officer

Duration : 1 day including Examination

Examination : The examination consists of  twenty  multiple-choice questions – online   examination

Pass percentage : 80%

What will students receive on successful completion? 

IBOEHS accredited Registered Ergonomic specialist Certificate
